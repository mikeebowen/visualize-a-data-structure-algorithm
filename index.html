Do You Know the highest Ski Resort in the US?

Try Selection Sort to Find Out

A Selection Sort Algorithm works by looking through a list and selecting the lowest (or highest) value and then moving it to the top of the list. Then the algorithm searches the remaining items in the list to find the lowest (or highest remaining) value and moves it up into place. This process is repeated until there are no more items to sort.
